About the Business

Specialties

Taste of Aloha from Da Hawaiian Soul where we blend a unique mix of both local Hawaiian and Soul food. We serve plate lunches and platters both. We also offer catering services. Mahalo for you kokua

History

Established in 2018.

Meet the Business Owner

I am a veteran of the United States Army. Born and raised on the island of Oahu. My brother Keone Laepa'a from Prodiver Maui helped me with my dream of building me a food truck that allowed me to serve both traditional local Hawaiian and Soul food. My husband and I work hard to offer courteous and prompt service the aloha way.

Stacey And Darryl A. Business Owner

As a native Hawaiian from Oahu and former veteran of the US Army stationed at Fort Bragg, NC. I was able to bring what I know from childhood and learned from the south to create a unique menu for all people. My husband and I blend well in that we can offer a great and tasty selection of unique food that is not offered anywhere around Delaware. We have brought the 50th state to the 1st state.
